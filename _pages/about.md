Hi! My name is Vishal Bakshi. I live in Portland, OR with my partner and our parrot Storm. I used to be the Director of Data Operations at [Measure Education](https://www.measureed.com) and currently am an Adjunct Faculty in the Engineering & Technology Department at [City College of San Francisco](http://ccsf.edu). I'm also an organizer at the [PyDataPDX meetup](https://www.meetup.com/PyData-PDX/events/), and volunteer with the Portland chapter of [DSA](http://portlanddsa.org/events). I spend my time learning Data Science (and tech in general), making music, writing lyrics, illustrating characters and typeface, playing League of Legends Teamfight Tactics, and pondering the future of our civilization, its systemic problems, and structural solutions. If you're into any or all of that, you can find me on Twitter [@extremelyvishal](http://twitter.com/extremelyvishal) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCNP2luBQbppj5_bBqhxD1kw/videos). Feel free to get in touch with me by email as well at vdbakshi@gmail.com.
